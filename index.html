Description

This Overlap shed range offers a great value storage space for your garden. The shed has a high roofline and a quality build setting itself apart from the competition. This garden building is supplied with styrene glazed windows, which are 100% shatterproof and a safer alternative to glass. The rigid frame and high quality rustic overlap board makes the shed strong and sturdy. These quality sheds have strong braced double doors with the roof and floor constructed of solid sheet materials giving the shed a durable structure. The apex roof is shielded from wood rot or fungal decay by the supplied mineral roofing felt.
This garden building is pre-treated with a water based timber treatment for protection during transit. In order to receive the full 10 year anti-rot guarantee, it is necessary that you treat the shed with a high quality timber preservative prior to or shortly after construction. This will protect the timber from weathering and will need to be repeated accordingly in relation to manufacturer's instructions. See Terms and Conditions for further information
Supplied with felt, fixings, and full instructions for DIY self-assembly. Manufactured in the UK

Features

Window Position

The window panel can be located on either the left or the right-hand side of the garden shed. We also have Windowless sheds available.

Free Delivery

We offer FREE delivery to most UK postcodes. To see if you are eligible for free delivery on this garden building please check here.

Roofing Felt

The garden building is supplied with FREE mineral roofing felt and galvanised clout nails.

Ledge Door

To add strength the garden building has Brace and Ledge door that also allows for a staple and hasp to be fitted to secure your shed.

Double Framed Corners

For added strength and stability the garden building features double framed corners.

Round Edge Framing

The Garden Sheds internal framing is planed and rounded making it resistant to moisture and helps avoid splinters to hands.

Floor Joists

To provide a sturdy support on your shed base this garden building comes with planed timber floor joists.

Portabase Available

We advise the use of a pressure treated portabase with this product to increase its longevity. See our information page for alternative bases.

Butterfly Catch

This garden building is supplied with a Butterfly catch to keep the door shut tight. Staple and pin available as an optional extra..

Cladding

This garden building has traditional overlap cladding with a rough sawn surface that makes the panel very absorbent which helps when apply a wood preserve.

Solid Sheet Roof and Floor

Hardwearing, stable, excellent moisture resistance and less prone to warping and layer separation than ply. Even surface without knots and doesn't split when nailed or screwed.

10yr Anti Rot

This Product carries the 10year Anti Rot guarantee as long as the garden building is well maintained.
